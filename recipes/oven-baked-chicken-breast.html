Oven Baked Chicken Breast

Description

Oven baked chicken breast is a protien rich meal ideal for anyone trying to gain muscle. Simple, tasty, and effective.

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless boneless chicken breast halves
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon coarse sea salt
  • 1 pinch Creole seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon chicken broth

Steps

  1. Preheat a oven to 400 degrees farenheit.
  2. Apply olive oil to chicken breast and season with salt and Creole seasoning.
  3. Place chicken in broiler pan and bake in oven for 10 minutes.
  4. Flip chicken and cook until the middle is not pink.
  5. Remove chicken and pour broth into pan.
  6. Scrap browned bits off and drizzle the pan sauce onto chicken.
  7. Enjoy!
