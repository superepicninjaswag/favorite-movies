Waffles

Description

Waffles are a staple breakfast item; quick and easy to make! Sweet and fluffy, they brighten anyones day.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • nonstick cooking spray

Steps

  1. Preheat a waffle iron.
  2. Whisk eggs until light and fluffy.
  3. Add flour, milk, and vegetable oil and mix to combine.
  4. Mix in sugar, baking powder, salt, and vanilla just until smooth, being careful not to overmix.
  5. Apply nonstick spray to waffle iron.
  6. Pour batter onto waffle iron and cook until golden brown and there is no more steam.
  7. Enjoy!
